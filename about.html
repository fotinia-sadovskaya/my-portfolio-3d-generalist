About me

3D Generalist from Dnipro, Ukraine

I am a 3D Generalist (junior) and graduated from the STEP Academy in Computer Graphics and IT Design. I also additionally studied English at the intermediate level. I have no experience in a startup and game development. But really, I want to create games and be part of a team of professionals who are passionate about their work. I really enjoy modeling, creating objects and characters.

I am well versed in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Figma, UI/UX, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Marvelous Designer, Marmoset Toolbag, TopoGun, 3DMax, Maya, Adobe Autor Effect, Adobe Media Encoder. My main focus is creating and animating 3D surface models and 3D characters.

I have a bachelor's degree in astronautics design engineering after graduating from the National University in Dnipro. I always liked to study, to learn something new. I love to model, apply non-standard design solutions. Easily, quickly and with pleasure I like to master new programs and gain new skills.

I have been taking photographs since childhood. I also love traveling, playing ping-pong, fishing, reading books and cycling. I would love to be a part of a wonderful experienced team and create great games!
